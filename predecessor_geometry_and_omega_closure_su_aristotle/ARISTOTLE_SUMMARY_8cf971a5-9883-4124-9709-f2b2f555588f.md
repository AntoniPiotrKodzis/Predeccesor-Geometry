## Notes
- The QIIT quotient constructors (Q1) in Definitions 4.1 and 6.1 are modeled via Lean's quotient types rather than higher inductive types, since Lean 4 does not natively support QIITs. The extensional collapse construction uses `Quotient` to achieve the same effect.
- Conjecture 8.2 (tree-stage cofinality invariance) is stated as a conjecture in the paper and is not formalized, as it is an open problem.
- All proofs compile cleanly with no `sorry` statements remaining.
